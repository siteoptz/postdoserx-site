Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive my meal plans?

Your personalized meal plans are generated instantly upon signup and can be accessed immediately in your dashboard.

Can I modify meal plans for my dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! Our meal plans can be customized for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and other dietary preferences.

Do you provide medical advice?

No, PostDoseRX provides meal planning suggestions only. We are not a medical service and do not provide medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider.

What GLP-1 medications do you support?

We support all major GLP-1 medications including Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound, Saxenda, Victoza, and Rybelsus.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no cancellation fees or long-term commitments.
