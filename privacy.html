Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

How we protect and use your personal information

Information We Collect

When you use PostDoseRx, we collect information that helps us provide better GLP-1 meal planning and support services:

Personal Information

  • Email address (for account access and communication)
  • Name (to personalize your experience)
  • GLP-1 medication information (to customize meal plans)
  • Health goals and dietary preferences
  • Progress tracking data (weight, energy levels, side effects)

Usage Information

  • App usage patterns and feature preferences
  • Meal plan selections and customizations
  • Community interaction data
  • Support requests and feedback

Technical Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and general location (for service optimization)
  • Analytics data to improve app performance

How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to provide and improve our GLP-1 support services:

  • Personalized Meal Planning: Create GLP-1-optimized meal plans based on your medication and preferences
  • Progress Tracking: Help you monitor your health journey and medication effectiveness
  • Community Features: Connect you with others on similar GLP-1 journeys (with your permission)
  • Customer Support: Respond to questions and provide technical assistance
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our platform
  • Important Communications: Send service updates, safety information, and account notifications

Information Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We only share information in these limited circumstances:

Healthcare Providers (With Your Permission)

If you choose to integrate with your healthcare provider, we can share progress reports and meal plan information to support your care.

Service Providers

We work with trusted third parties who help us operate our service:

  • Cloud hosting providers (for secure data storage)
  • Analytics services (to understand app usage)
  • Email service providers (for account communications)
  • Payment processors (for subscription handling)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety and rights of our users.

Data Security

Your health information is extremely sensitive, and we protect it accordingly:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access user data
  • Regular Audits: We conduct security reviews and updates
  • HIPAA Compliance: We follow healthcare data protection standards
  • Secure Infrastructure: We use industry-leading cloud security practices

Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your personal information:

Access and Updates

  • View and update your profile information anytime
  • Download a copy of your data
  • Correct any inaccurate information

Communication Preferences

  • Choose which emails you receive
  • Opt out of marketing communications (service emails will continue)
  • Manage push notification settings

Account Deletion

You can delete your account anytime. This will remove your personal information within 30 days (some data may be retained for legal compliance).

Medical Disclaimer

Important: PostDoseRx provides meal planning suggestions and general information. We are not providing medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before making changes to your medication regimen or diet.

Our meal plans and recommendations are based on general best practices for GLP-1 users and should not replace professional medical guidance.

Children's Privacy

PostDoseRx is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately.

International Users

If you're outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and stored in the US. By using our service, you consent to this transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.
